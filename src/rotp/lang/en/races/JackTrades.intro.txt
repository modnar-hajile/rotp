The [race] Technocracy
The year is 12321...

You sit at the Table of Philosophers as the unanimously acknowledged Grand Philosopher of the [race] Technocracy. After a period of societal renaissance in all fields, the people stand united to begin anew under the guidance of knowledge.
Knowing that you are the one destined to guide your people into the cosmos to claim whatever waits to be discovered therein, you direct your advisors to complete work on functional starships as soon as possible. 
Your people have the gift of heart and body and mind and strength and charisma and cunning, their versatility will easily overcome any specialized resistance they find in the depths of the galaxy. 
After fifteen months of preparation, the time has come to compel the balanced might of the Technocracy forward. The galaxy will marvel at the effectiveness of the Jack of All Trades...
